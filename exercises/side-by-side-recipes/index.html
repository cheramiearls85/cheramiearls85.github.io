Korean Inspired Tuna Melt

  • preptime:10 minutes
  • Total Time:20 minutes
  • Cook Time:10 minutes
  • Yields:3 sandwiches

This Korean-inspired tuna melt delivers everything you want in a great sandwich: crisp bread, a creamy filling, and cheese that melts perfectly into every layer. The tuna stays bold and savory, the kimchi adds just enough depth, and the garlic butter ensures a golden, crunchy exterior. Simple, satisfying, and full of flavor.
